# Project Ideas

## Projects to use in this system
- **Posture-Sense:** PC webcam-based posture detection and ergonomic correction.
- **Heart-Sense:** Cardiovascular anomaly detection and systemic health tracking via smart watch biometrics.
- **EchoSense:** Adaptive hearing aid and contextual memory assistant utilizing the smartphone and Pixel Buds.
- **Muse-Feedback:** Real-time EEG brainwave monitoring, cognitive state tracking, and immersive biofeedback.
- **Dynamic-Pill-Scheduler:** Personal OS acting as the central data aggregator and scheduler using the Pixel Watch.
- **Story-Generator** A completely local, multi-PC story generation tool that transforms interactive fiction into a professional Cinematic Experience. Generate proactive narratives, maintaining a living world with independent NPCs, dynamic weather, and multi-layered Visual Novel aesthetics.

## Edge Sensors (Used in the projects above)
- **Pixel Watch 3:** Heart rate, HRV, SpO2, sleep stages, motion
- **Pixel Buds:** Environmental audio, speech context 
- **Muse Headband:** EEG brainwaves, head motion
- **PC Webcam:** Visual skeletal tracking
- **PC 1:** ai context jobs
- **PC 2:** Heavy ai context jobs
- **Pixel 9 XL Pro Camera:** Visual context and object/pill logging
- **Pixel 9 XL:** Local gemma based ai

## Actionable Biofeedback (Output)
- **Immersive Visuals:** PC monitor (like the 3D Muse interface) shifting to guide focus and subtly correct posture.
- **Auditory Nudges:** Pixel buds delivering binaural beats to lower Beta waves or whispered posture reminders via EchoSense.
- **Haptic Alerts:** Pixel Watch tapping to enforce pill schedules or trigger paced breathing exercises.

## Unified Master Dashboard
- Build a comprehensive "Omni-Health" frontend that acts as the command center, combining the 3D brainwave particle field, posture skeletons, and heart rhythm charts.
